# Security Awareness & Training Policy

**Policy Number:** SEC-011  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** 2026-06-25  
**Owner:** Security Officer / CEO  
**SOC 2 Criteria:** CC2.2, CC1.4, CC1.5

---

## 1. Purpose

Ensure all MerchantsBI personnel have the security knowledge necessary to recognize threats,
follow security policies, and protect company and customer data.

## 2. Scope

All full-time employees, part-time employees, and contractors with access to MerchantsBI systems
or data.

## 3. Security Awareness Training

### 3.1 New Hire Training
All new employees and contractors complete security awareness training within **14 days** of
start date. Training covers:
- MerchantsBI security policies (this policy library).
- Phishing and social engineering recognition.
- Password and MFA requirements.
- Data classification and handling.
- Acceptable use of company systems and AI tools.
- Incident reporting procedures.

Completion is documented in the onboarding checklist.

### 3.2 Annual Refresher Training
All personnel complete a security refresher annually. Content updated to reflect:
- New threats and recent incidents (internal or industry-wide).
- Policy changes from the past year.
- Emerging risks specific to MerchantsBI's tech stack (cloud, AI APIs, etc.).

### 3.3 Role-Specific Training
Personnel with elevated access or security responsibilities receive additional training:

| Role | Additional Training |
|------|-------------------|
| Engineers (production access) | Secure coding practices, secrets management, OWASP Top 10 |
| Security Officer | Incident response, SOC 2 controls, threat intelligence |
| All staff | Phishing simulation (semi-annual) |

## 4. Phishing Simulations

MerchantsBI runs **semi-annual** simulated phishing campaigns. Employees who click on simulation
links receive immediate just-in-time training. Aggregate results (not individual names) are
reported to leadership. Persistent failure triggers mandatory one-on-one security coaching.

## 5. Policy Acknowledgment

All personnel acknowledge they have read and will comply with MerchantsBI security policies:
- At onboarding (new hire).
- Annually (refresher cycle).
- Upon material policy change.

Acknowledgments are stored in the HR system or a designated document tracker.

## 6. Security Communications

The Security Officer communicates security updates to all staff:
- **Ad hoc alerts** for urgent threats (critical CVEs affecting our stack, industry-wide incidents).
- **Monthly digest** summarizing security events, policy reminders, and upcoming reviews.

## 7. Training Records

Training completion records are retained for a minimum of **3 years** and are available for
SOC 2 audit review.

## 8. Measuring Effectiveness

The Security Officer tracks:
- Training completion rates (target: 100% within required windows).
- Phishing simulation click rates (target: declining year-over-year).
- Security incident counts attributable to human error.

Results are reviewed annually and inform content updates.
